In the series India in Bangladesh  - 2022 (2022/23), the match between India and Bangladesh was played at Zahur Ahmed Chowdhury Stadium, Chattogram, Bangladesh from 2022-12-14 to 2022-12-18. India were captained by KL Rahul, while Bangladesh were led by Shakib Al Hasan.
India won the toss and elected to bat first. Debutants in this match were: Zakir Hasan.
