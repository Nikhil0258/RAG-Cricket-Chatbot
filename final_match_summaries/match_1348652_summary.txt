In the series Australia in India  - 2023 (2022/23), the match between Australia and India was played at Vidarbha Cricket Association Stadium, Nagpur, India from 2023-02-09 to 2023-02-11. Australia were captained by Pat Cummins, while India were led by Rohit Sharma.
Australia won the toss and elected to bat first. Debutants in this match were: Srikar Bharat, Suryakumar Yadav, Todd Murphy.

Innings Summary:
Australia 1 innings: 177/10. Top scorer was Marnus Labuschagne (49.0), while Ravindra Jadeja (5 wickets) led the bowling.
Australia 2 innings: 91.0/10.0. Top scorer was Steven Smith (25.0), while Ravichandran Ashwin (5 wickets) led the bowling.
India 1 innings: 400.0/10.0. Top scorer was Rohit Sharma (120.0), while Todd Murphy (7 wickets) led the bowling.

Final Result: India won by an innings and 132 runs. Ravindra Jadeja was named Player of the Match.
